About Me

Hi, my name is Teddy. I'm Greek, and always looking for new experiences. My mantra is YOLO, you only live once. Skydiving, mountain climbing, hiking, and more. My YOLO list for 2019 is as follows: snowboarding, paragliding and more mountain climbing. Growing up eating greek food has turned me into a foodie and I'm thankful for that!

I used to fix computers since age 10. I didn't have a good experience with technical support so I took it upon myself to learn troubleshooting steps. The computer manual was there and I started reading. Fast forward a couple of years and I graduated with Computer Science degree at Queens College.

Finding a job wasn't easy... Entry level positions with 3 years experience required while unreasonable were what I could find. After a few months of searching I finally found a technical support position at Columbia Psychiatry. It was a volunteer job but I was greatful to earn some experiance.

After 6 months, I was offered a job and from there I was working above my pay grade. Building image servers, MBAM servers and assistiing with administrative tasks. I was up for anything. I was very lucky that there were co-workers that appreciated my help and would help me in return. Unfortunately, opportunities for advancement within were not readily available and I had no choice but to look for another job. Finally, within about 2 months I recieved three offers for an administrator positions. I currently work as a systems engineer/administrator for a Managed Service Provider. We manage over 180 clients, mainly in finance.
